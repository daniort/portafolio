About me

My name is Daniel Ortiz Castro, I am a Computer Systems Engineer, specializing in Software Development. I was born in Oaxaca. Mexico, and my goal is to be part of a team that solves problems to help society and to grow with that.
I was part of the team of the Free Software Laboratory in the city of Zacatecas, that opened the doors to new projects as a freelancer.
Recently I have been working with hybrid technologies for mobile applications and I consider that currently that is my strength, although I also have knowledge in web development.
